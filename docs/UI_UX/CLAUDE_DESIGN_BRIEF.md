# VOD Auctions — Design System "Vinyl Culture"

Copy-paste briefing for Claude Design / Stitch / external designers.

---

**Project:** Auction platform for rare industrial / experimental / electronic music vinyl, cassettes and CDs. Premium, dark, editorial. Audience: serious record collectors.

## Typography

- **Headings** (Hero, H1, H2): `DM Serif Display`, weight 400
- **Body, UI, H3 (semibold):** `DM Sans`
- Both via Google Fonts. Letter-spacing on hero: `-0.01em`.

## Voice & Feel

Editorial, curated, serious. Not flashy. Think a high-end auction catalogue meeting a 90s industrial record sleeve. Generous whitespace, serif headlines, gold accents used sparingly as highlights — never as filler.
