The operators take the protection of personal data very seriously. Personal data is treated confidentially and in accordance with statutory regulations.

You may withdraw consent at any time via informal email notification. Prior processing legality remains unaffected.

For direct marketing, you may object at any time to personal data processing for advertising purposes. After objection, data will not be used for direct marketing.

GDPR violations permit complaints to a supervisory authority, especially where you reside, work, or where violation occurred.

### Right to Data Portability

You may obtain data processed based on consent or contract fulfillment in standard, machine-readable format.

This site uses SSL/TLS encryption for secure transmission of confidential content such as orders or enquiries. Encrypted connections show "https://" and a lock symbol.

## 3. Data Collection on This Website

### Cookies

The site uses cookies. Cookies do not cause damage and do not contain viruses. Most are session cookies deleted automatically after your visit. Others persist until deletion, enabling browser recognition on return visits.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in error-free, optimized service).

### Server Log Files

The provider automatically collects and stores the following:
- Browser type and version
- Operating system used
- Referrer URL
- Hostname of accessing computer
- Server request time
- IP address

Data is not merged with other sources. Legal basis: Art. 6 (1)(f) GDPR.

### Registration on This Website

Registered users' data is retained while registered, then deleted. Legal basis: Art. 6 (1)(a) GDPR (consent).

### Comment Function

The comment function stores: comment content, creation timestamp, email address, and username. IP addresses are stored to enable action against rights violations. Users may subscribe to comment notifications; unsubscribing is possible at any time.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1)(a) GDPR (consent).

### Customer and Contract Data

Personal data is collected and processed only as required for establishing or modifying contractual relationships. Legal basis: Art. 6 (1)(b) GDPR. Customer data is deleted after order completion or business relationship termination, subject to statutory retention periods.
