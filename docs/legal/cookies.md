# Cookie Policy

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are used to make the website functional and to improve the user experience.

## Which Cookies Do We Use?

### Essential Cookies (always active)

These cookies are required for the website to function and cannot be disabled.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `sb-*-auth-token` | Supabase authentication — maintains your login session | Session / 1 week |
| `sb-*-auth-token-code-verifier` | PKCE security token for secure authentication | Session |
| `vod-cookie-consent` | Stores your cookie consent preference | Permanent |

### Analytics Cookies (optional)

These cookies are only set if you accept analytics in our cookie consent banner. They help us understand how visitors use the site.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics — distinguishes unique visitors | 2 years |
| `_ga_*` | Google Analytics 4 — maintains session state | 2 years |

### Marketing Cookies (optional)

These cookies are used for behavior tracking, personalized recommendations, and potential future advertising. They are only set if you explicitly consent to marketing cookies in our cookie consent banner.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `sib_cuid` | Brevo — unique visitor identifier for behavior tracking | 13 months |
| `sib_sesn` | Brevo — session tracking for page view counting | Session |
| `_fbp` | Facebook Pixel — tracks visits for ad targeting (future) | 3 months |
| `_gcl_*` | Google Ads — conversion tracking and remarketing (future) | 90 days |

## Managing Your Preferences

When you first visit VOD Auctions, a cookie consent banner allows you to choose which optional cookies you accept. You can accept or reject analytics and marketing cookies independently. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for the website to function.

To change your preference, clear your browser cookies for vod-auctions.com and reload the page — the consent banner will appear again.
