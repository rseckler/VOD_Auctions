## 2. General Information and Mandatory Information

### Consent Revocation

Users may withdraw consent at any time via informal email notification without affecting prior lawful processing.

### Right to Object

Users may object to data processing under Art. 21 GDPR in specific circumstances, including direct advertising.

### Data Portability

Users can request commonly-used, machine-readable formats of automatically-processed data based on consent or contract fulfillment.

### Encryption

This website uses SSL or TLS encryption for secure transmission. Encrypted connections display "https://" and a lock icon. Payment information is transmitted exclusively through encrypted SSL/TLS connections.

### Data Subject Rights

Users may request information about archived data, its sources, recipients, and processing purposes. They may demand rectification, blocking, or deletion. To exercise rights, contact: frank@vod-records.com

Users may demand restrictions on data processing when:

- Data is no longer needed but required for legal claims

## 3. Data Collection on Website

### Cookies

Cookies do not cause damage and do not contain viruses. They enhance user-friendliness, effectiveness, and security. Most cookies are session-based and auto-delete upon site exit. Others persist until manual deletion.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in error-free, optimized service).

### Server Log Files

Automatically collected information includes:
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Referrer URL
- Accessing computer hostname
- Server request time
- IP address

This data is not merged with other sources. Legal basis: Art. 6 (1)(f) GDPR.

### Contact Form

Information submitted through contact forms is stored for inquiry handling. Legal basis: Art. 6 (1)(a) GDPR (consent). Data is retained until deletion is requested or the inquiry purpose is fulfilled.

### Email, Telephone, or Fax Requests

Contact requests are stored and processed for handling purposes without third-party sharing absent user consent. Legal basis: Art. 6 (1)(b) or Art. 6 (1)(a) and (f) GDPR.

### Website Registration

Users may register for additional functions. Email addresses are used to notify users of important changes. Legal basis: Art. 6 (1)(a) GDPR (consent).

### Customer and Contract Data

Personal data is collected only as necessary for establishing, organizing, or modifying legal relationships. Legal basis: Art. 6 (1)(b) GDPR. Customer data is deleted upon order completion or business relationship termination, subject to statutory retention obligations.

### Third-Party Data Sharing for Contract Fulfillment

Personal data may be shared with third parties only when necessary for contract handling (e.g. shipment companies or payment processors). Further transfers require express user consent.
