07 — Strategy

The Invite Wave Strategy

Controlled rollout by audience trust level. Maximum quality, minimum churn at each stage.

Password gate removed — RSE-78

Why Waves?

Controlled rollout means we can fix issues at each stage before the next cohort arrives. No fire-drills at public launch.

Wave 1 Priority

tape-mag buyers are already proven customers with payment history. Highest conversion likelihood, lowest support burden.

Scarcity Signal

The invite system creates perceived exclusivity — collectors talk to other collectors. Organic word-of-mouth amplification.
